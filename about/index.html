About

2019-04-27

I am an IT specialist located in Denver, Colorado, with a strong interest in cyber-security. This blog is intended as a way to demonstrate my growth as a person and as a professional as I grow my skills and myself, as well as showcase my creations. The blog is focused on three focii:

  • Personal - Personal entries will be intended to demonstrate growth as a person as I move forward in life and deal with past issues.

  • Professional - Professional entries will be intended to demonstrate growth in my career and my technical skills.

  • Creative - Creative entries will be intended to showcase things that I make myself. These may be literary, musical, or physical works of art.
