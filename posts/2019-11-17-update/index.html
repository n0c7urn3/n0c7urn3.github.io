Quick update since August

2019-11-17
#life update 

It has been 3 months. And before that it had been 2. I’m sure not updating this as much as I’d like to, but it seems that when I have the time, I don’t have the energy, and when I have the energy, I don’t have the time, and when I do have both, I just don’t know what to say.

I finished my studying for CompTIA CySA+ back in September and have plenty of notes that I plan to turn into posts. ’ve also been participating in Operation Chimera by Security Blue Team, which is a series of blue team training courses that go over some theory, and then have the participants perform some hands on challenges surrounding the coursework, and it’s been a lot of fun, and has given me plenty of ideas for additional posts.

Additionally, today, I shut down my VPS and moved the blog over to Github to save myself a few dollars per month. You can still find it at the same address, though. That was a bit of a chore, since I changed machines and apparently Hugo has updated recently and made some pretty extreme changes from what I was used to previously, plus the difficulty of learning git in the process.

I’m also planning on changing the categories from “Personal,” “Professional,” and “Creative to something along the lines of “Life,” “Technical,” and “Creative.” I still don’t have any creative works uploaded, or completed, but I still want to do so once things have settled.

Offline, I’m moving to my team’s swing shift soon, which should give me a more compatible schedule with my partner, as well as allow me a more stable sleep schedule if I can get to bed before the sun comes up (I’ll also enjoy not driving into the sunrise on my way home). More than that, I have some plans I’m working on executing, so stay tuned for that.

I think that’s about all I got for the moment. Stay frosty!
