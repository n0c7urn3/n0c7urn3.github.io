Kerberos Authentication Protocol

2020-02-24
#kerberos  #CySA 
“As a twelfth labour Herakles was to fetch Kerberos from Haides’s realm. Kerberos had three dog-heads, a serpent for a tail, and along his back the heads of all kinds of snakes.” Pseudo-Apollodorus, Bibliotheca 2.122 Kerberos, often translated to Cerberus, is the three headed guard dog at the gates of Hades in Greek mythology. Similar in function, the Kerberos authentication protocol was borne of MIT’s Project Athena, which was designed to make computing more available to students through distributed computing.
