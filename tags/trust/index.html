Trusting Your Gut

2019-06-06
#trust  #judgement  #intuition 
I think we all have a specific moment in which we learn to trust that little voice in the back of our heads; the one that tells us, “You should run.” Sometimes it’s wrong, and you have to look at the situation to figure that out, but sometimes listening to it is the best choice you can make. To provide some background, both of my parents were recovering alcoholics and drug addicts, and both attended our local Alcoholics Anonymous club (henceforth referred to as “the Club”).
