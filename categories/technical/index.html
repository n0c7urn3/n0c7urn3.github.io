Bachelor’s Degree Capstone Project: Incident Response Plan part 4

2020-01-19
#incident response 
Section 4: Asset Security Management Security practitioners face many issues when determining best how to secure an organization, but asset management is among the most challenging and the most critical. One cannot protect what one does not know exists. A properly executed IT Asset Management program could mean the difference between a well run network and a hodgepodge of poor documentation that creates easily access footholds for attackers. At a minimum, an IT Asset Management program should track the authorized equipment on the network and the information that describes it, including location, user info, configurations, and roles and responsibility (Brien, 2016).
